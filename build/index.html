Diu

Diu Island, connected by a bridge to Gujarat's southern coast, resonates with Portuguese heritage. Its notable architectural gems encompass three churches and a seafront fort. Beyond the bustling tourist-filled waterfront, the town streets offer a serene and immaculate atmosphere. Notably, alcohol is permissible on this charming island.
